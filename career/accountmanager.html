Account Manager

TITLE: Account Manager

LOCATION:  Tbilisi Georgia

JOB DESCRIPTION: McCann Tbilisi is looking for a responsible and experienced, highly motivated, enthusiastic person on Account Manager position.

JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Receiving and responding to initial requests / briefs from local and foreign clients;
  • Organizing meetings with clients, discussing and identifying their advertising requirements;
  • Review all major deliverables, investigate clients’ needs and develop proposals;
  • Preparation and development of new projects;
  • Work closely with project team members to manage project status, to identify potential problems or opportunities during the project, and to develop appropriate action if any;
  • Ensure that all processes and procedures are completed, quality standards are met;
  • Provide regular two-way communication between client and team, provide strong team representation and set proper client expectations;
  • Full knowledge of the company’s capabilities and services, and their effective communication with customers;
  • Submission of offers / projects to customers for their approval and / or modification according to customer comments;
  • Plan budget and how resources are allocated across the project; manage campaign costs and invoicing for clients;
  • Prepare a tender offer with the employees of the Agency and participate in tenders for its profit;
  • Coordination with financial and legal departments in frame of projects, providing necessary information timely;
  • Provide and seek feedback.

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:

  • University degree in Management, Marketing or other related fields;
  • At least 2 years of work experience in marketing and advertising;
  • Review all major deliverables, investigate clients’ needs and develop proposals;
  • Strong presentation and negotiation skills;
  • Good organizational and time management skills;
  • Good communication skills for working with a range of colleagues and clients;
  • Ability to motivate teams;
  • A willingness to work under pressure;
  • Good business sense and the ability to work with budgets;
  • Extremely detail oriented;
  • Strong knowledge of English;
  • Good knowledge of Russian;
